About Akasa Virtual Airline

Akasa Virtual Airline is a community-driven virtual airline inspired by Akasa Air. Our goal is to recreate realistic airline operations while keeping the experience fun and accessible for pilots of all skill levels.

We operate real-world routes across India using a modern Boeing 737 fleet, supported by detailed route planning, structured operations, and an active pilot community.
